A little bit about me

A born and raised Canadian, now living in sunny Arizona, I am in the pursuit of discovering a way to integrate and balance my work life with my personal life. The main intent of this journey is to support my number one passion, TRAVEL! To this day, my entire working career, I have had to adhere to the brick and mortar, "9-5", scheduled work model. I am ready to break free! I am embarking upon a new journey, studying for a career in coding. Coding

offers the freedom, versatility, and creativity I am looking for in my work. At this stage in my life, being fulfilled professionally and personally is a high priority. Join me in this endeavor, right from the beginning, as I navigate my way through different types and levels of coding. Watch as I break free from the chains of a structured traditional workday to workdays filled passion for my ”job” and the accompanying flexibility and freedom to travel!

Real Talk

My website is meant to be a reflection of my journey as I make a significant transition my in life. Putting my all into what I am doing and trusting the process for a positive outcome.
